The Experience Gap in Nigeria: How Pekamy Freshminds is Bridging the Divide"

"Skilled But Unfit for work? We're Changing that"

In today's world, acquiring knowlegde is just the first step, applying it is where the real value lies. Unfortunately, the traditional academic system often stops at the theory. Many students leave school equipped with certificates but lacking real world experience or the motivation to keep learning. On the flip side, employers demand experience but rarely provide the platforms, such as internships to help build it.

The gap between education and employability has created a frustrating loop. Youths feel stuck, organizations are dissatisfied, and potential of a vibrant workforce goes untapped
