About PFM

PFM is a growing community built to unlock career opportunities for individuals at every stage of their journey.
Whether you're just starting out, looking to gain experience, or seeking to showcase your skills.Pekamy Freshminds bridges the gap between talent and opportunity.


How it Works

At Pekamy Freshminds,we bridges the gap between talents and opportunity through a structured, hands-on process.

  • Submit Your Details:Talents send their full names, skillsets, Job interest,location,and phone number.

  • Screening & Talent Webinars: We organize mandatory webinars to guide talents on what to expect, how to stand out,and how the Pekamy Entry Track (PET) works.

  • PET Programs: A structured onboarding phase where top-tier candidates are fast-tracked (minimum of 3 months), while intermediate candidates continue longer based on performance and learning pace.

  • Screening & Evaluation:Candidates are assessed for, skills, communication, and, work-readiness through task and guided mentorship.

  • Pairing with Opportunities:After evaluation, successful talents are matched with internships, freelance gigs, or job placements.
