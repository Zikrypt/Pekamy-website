With the launch of PET, here is a clear breakdown of the process each participants will go through:

  1. CV Submission: Participants are expected to submit their updated CV'S through the provided form. This marks the beginning of the onboarding process.
  2. Screening & Review: The screening team will assess all submitted CV'S to evaluate relevant skills, interest, and alignment with potential roles.
  3. Clarity Call: Shortlisted candidates will be invited to a Clarity call and Cv review, participants will be assigned to a specific tier. This helps us provide the right level of support and track growth appropriately.
  4. Tier Classification: Based on responses during the Clarity Call and the CV review, Participants will be assignedto a secific tier. This will help us provide the right level of support, and track growth appropriately.
  5. Niche-Based Grouping: Participants will be then grouped based on thier chosen niche (e.g, design, virtual assistance, project management,etc). Each group will be assigned a supervisor who will serve as their guide throughout the process.
  6. Mock Project Assignment: Participants will be given a mock project tailored to their niche. This task simulates real-life work scenarios and allows participants to demonstrate their skills in a practical way.
  7. Performance Review & Feedback: Supervisors will review submitted projects and provide constructive feedback based on execution, problem-solving, communication, and attention to detail.
  8. Learning Support & Supervision: Throughout the program, participants wil have access to relevant learning resources and ongoing support from their supervisors. Regular check-ins will also be conducted to track progress and address and challenges.
  9. Live Project Opportunities: Participants who show strong potential during the mock phase may be considered for live projects or matched with external opportunities, depending on performance and availability.
